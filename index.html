Family Gathering

The Yandt family gathers every summer at a cabin in Priest Lake, Idaho. This year, in 2023, a new "Yandt" was welcomed to the family: Charlie O'Hara. A Typical family week includes a themed party, hanging out on the dock, and lots of card games.
